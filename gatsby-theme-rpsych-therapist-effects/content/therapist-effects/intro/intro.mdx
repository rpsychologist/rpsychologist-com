Although therapist effects have been discussed for as long psychotherapy research has existed, they seem poorly understood from a causal inference perspective, and interpretations are mostly based on "variance-explained" measures that confound non-causal and causal factors. This page is an supplemental interactive visualization of the idea proposed in the paper: Magnusson, K. (2023, in preparation). [*A Causal Inference Perspective on Therapist Effects.*](https://psyarxiv.com/f7mvz)
